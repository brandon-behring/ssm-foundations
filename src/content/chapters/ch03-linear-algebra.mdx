# Linear algebra for sequence models: structured matrices and conditioning

<NoteBox title="Scaffolded">
Authoring in progress (2026-05-24). Foundation chapter for the SSM duality lens (Ch 9) and for B's gate-architecture analysis.
</NoteBox>

The structured matrices that show up throughout sequence-model architectures — Toeplitz (convolution), semiseparable (state-space duality), Vandermonde and Cauchy (S4 kernel construction) — and the conditioning properties that govern their numerical behavior. This is the linear-algebra foundation the rest of the book treats as background.

Foundation for: Ch 7 (HiPPO basis conditioning), Ch 8 (LTI SSMs), Ch 9 (SSD framework).

Source weeks in [post_transformers/roadmap.md](https://github.com/brandon-behring/post_transformers/blob/main/roadmap.md): W04 (structured-matrix view of S4), W08 (semiseparable matrices in SSD).

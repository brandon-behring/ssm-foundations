# Stability theory: Lyapunov, A-stability, BIBO

<NoteBox title="Scaffolded">
Authoring in progress (2026-05-24). Foundation chapter for C1 (A-stability for the symplectic vs exp-trapezoidal comparison) and W18 Lyapunov analysis.
</NoteBox>

Three notions of stability that the curriculum needs as distinct objects: Lyapunov exponents (asymptotic trajectory divergence), A-stability (regions of absolute stability for ODE integrators), and BIBO stability (bounded-input-bounded-output for LTI systems). Each surfaces differently in the SSM literature; this chapter establishes the vocabulary.

Foundation for: C1 symplectic pilot, A3 dynamical diagnostics, W18 Lyapunov analysis.

Source weeks in [post_transformers/roadmap.md](https://github.com/brandon-behring/post_transformers/blob/main/roadmap.md): W01 (QR-based Lyapunov), W05 (S4D eigenvalue analysis), W18 (Lyapunov spectra of trained SSMs).

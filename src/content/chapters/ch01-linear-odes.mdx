# Linear ODEs as state-space systems

<NoteBox title="Scaffolded">
Authoring in progress (2026-05-24). Foundation chapter for the C1 symplectic-integrator pilot.

This chapter introduces continuous-time linear systems as the mathematical foundation for everything that follows. The state-space form $\dot{h}(t) = Ah(t) + Bu(t)$ recurs throughout the curriculum — as the continuous source of S4 (Ch 8), as the continuous limit of selective SSMs (Ch 9), and as the object whose structure preservation drives the C1 pilot (Ch 6 + research).

Foundation for: C1 symplectic pilot, B two-timescale benchmarks.
